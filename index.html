Replenish Day Risk Assessment

This quiz will help you decide how much freedom you can allow yourself on your Replenish Day. Answer each question honestly to find out whether you should take a more flexible or more structured approach during your break from OMAD.

1. Do you often eat past the point of fullness when enjoying your favorite foods?

2. If you plan a meal or treat in advance, do you usually stick to the plan?

3. When you're around social food situations (e.g. parties or gatherings), do you tend to lose control or binge eat?

4. Have you ever told yourself “this is just one treat” but ended up having multiple unplanned meals or snacks?

5. After a Replenish Day or a cheat meal in the past, have you struggled to get back on track the next day?

6. Do you feel confident that you could enjoy a Replenish Day and stop eating when comfortably full?

7. When food is available or in front of you, do you feel compelled to finish it even if you're no longer hungry?

8. Do you tend to eat based on emotion (boredom, stress, celebration) more than hunger?
